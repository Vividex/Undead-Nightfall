How to Play

  • Survive as long as possible against the undead horde.
  • Defeat bosses to level up and increase your strength.
  • Use sword attacks, Fireball, and Chain Lightning to control the battlefield.
  • Build a sword kill streak to trigger Berserk Mode.
  • Collect health, mana, and power-up drops to stay alive.
